Tango Friday marathon icon creation session:

http://tango.freedesktop.org/Tango_Fridays#Friday_March_30th

http://blog.vybiral.info/2007/03/30/gtk-stock-icons-marathon-is-over/

Jakub Steiner, Steven Garrity, Tuomas Kuosmanen, Andreas Nilsson, Lapo Calamandrei, Josef Vybiral, Hylke Bons, Charavel Olivier, Kalle Persson and David Prieto.
